Travel & Lodging

Below you’ll find information on how to get from the three major New York City airports to Williamsburg, Brooklyn, as well as recommended accommodations for our wedding.

Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

From Newark, you can take the AirTrain to Newark Liberty International Airport Station, then transfer to NJ Transit to Penn Station. From there, take the L subway line to Bedford Ave in Williamsburg. Alternatively, a taxi or ride-share takes about 1-1.5 hours depending on traffic.

LaGuardia Airport (LGA)

From LaGuardia, you can take the Q70-SBS bus to Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Ave, then transfer to the E subway line to Court Square and switch to the G line to Metropolitan Ave. A taxi or ride-share typically takes 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on traffic.

John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)

From JFK, take the AirTrain to Howard Beach Station, then transfer to the A subway line to Broadway Junction and switch to the L line to Bedford Ave. A taxi or ride-share can take between 1-1.5 hours depending on traffic.
